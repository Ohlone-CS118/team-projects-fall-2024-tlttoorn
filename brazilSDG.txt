Brazil:

No Poverty:
Challenges:
- In 2024, 11.53% of the population was making $3.65/day (living in extreme poverty)

Zero Hunger:
Achieved:
- Exports of hazardous pesticides
- 3.1% of children under 5 y/o considered underweight for their height(s)
- 4.7% of population is considered undernourished
Major Challenges Remaining:
- 28.14% of the adult population is considered obese (BMI>=30)
- More efficient use of Nitrogen (on a scale from 0-1, Brazil scored 0.44)

Good Health and Well-Being:
Achieved: 
- Births attended by a skilled doctor is at 98.20%
- Mortality rate of children under 5 y/o is 14.03 per every 1000 live births
- Subjective health is rated as 6.55/10 (survey based)
Challenges remaining:
- Age standardized death rates due to pollution is 29 per 100000 people
- Maternal - Mortality ratio of 72.15 per 100000 live births
	
Quality Education:
Achieved: 
- Literacy rate: 99.28% of population of ages 15-24
Challenges:
- Students enrolled in pre-primary school programs is ~89.03% of kids aged 4-6
- Primary school enrollment rate is 94.77%

Climate Action:
Achieved:
- Greenhouse Gas emissions in imports is 0.96 tCO2 per capita
Major Challenges Remaining:
- CO2 emissions from fossil fuel exports is ~630.48 kg per capita
- CO2 emissions from fossil fuels is about 2.25 tons of CO2 per capita
