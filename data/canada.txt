Canada:

No Poverty:
Achieved: 
- In 2024, 0.28% of the population was making $3.65/day (living in extreme poverty)
Major Challenges remaining: 
- 10.5% of the population lives in poverty after taxes and transfers

Zero Hunger:
Achieved:
- 0.7% of children under 5 y/o considered underweight for their height(s)
- 2.5% of population is considered undernourished
Major Challenges Remaining:
- 26.23% of the adult population is considered obese (BMI>=30)
- More efficient use of Nitrogen (on a scale from 0-1, Canada scored 0.76)
- Exports of hazardous pesticides 

Good Health and Well-Being:
Achieved: 
- Maternal - Mortality ratio of 11.01 per 100000 live births
- Age standardized death rates due to pollution is 8 per 100000 people
- 4.7 traffic deaths per 100000 people
- Subjective health is rated as 6.84/10 (survey based)
Challenges remaining:
- 11.47 year gap in life expectancy at birth
- 97.6% of live births are attended by a skilled health personnel

Quality Education:
Achieved: 
- (Lower secondary education) Middle school completion rate is 98.84%
- 66.97% of people of age 25-34 attain a higher education (i.e. Bachelor's, Masters, etc.)
Challenges:
- Students who are sub-par in math is 21.63% of 15-year-olds
- Net primary school enrollment is 96.29%

Climate Action:
Major Challenges Remaining:
- CO2 emissions from fossil fuels is about 14.31 tons of CO2 per capita
- Greenhouse Gas emissions is 6.85 tCO2 per capita
- CO2 emissions from exports is 17208.68 kg/capita
