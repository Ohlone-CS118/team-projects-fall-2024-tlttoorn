India:

No Poverty:
Achieved: 
- In 2024, 1.18% of the population was making $2.15/day (living in extreme poverty)
Major Challenges remaining:
- 7.06% of the population was making $3.65/day (living in extreme poverty)

Zero Hunger:
Achieved:
- 7.27% of the adult population is considered obese (BMI>=30)
- Exports of hazardous pesticides 
Major Challenges Remaining:
- 35.5% of children under 5 y/o considered underweight for their height(s)
- 16.6% of population is considered undernourished
- More efficient use of Nitrogen (on a scale from 0-1, India scored 0.77)

Good Health and Well-Being:
Achieved: 
- Only .05 per 1000 people are infected with HIV
- The percentage of surviving infants that received the 2 WHO vaccines is 95%
Challenges remaining:
- Age standardized death rates due to pollution is 139 per 100000 people
- 14.6 traffic deaths per 100000 people
- Maternal - Mortality ratio of 102.7 per 100000 live births
- Subjective health is rated as 4.68/10 (survey based)
	
Quality Education:
Achieved: 
- Net primary school enrollment rate is 91.39%
- 65% of people of age 25-34 attain a higher education (i.e. Bachelor's, Masters, etc.)
- Participation in pre-primary school programs is 91.39% of children aged 4-6
Challenges:
- (Lower secondary education) Middle school completion rate is 87.24%

Climate Action:
Achieved:
- Greenhouse Gas emissions is 0.4 tCO2 per capita
Major Challenges Remaining:
- CO2 emissions from fossil fuels is about 2 tons of CO2 per capita
