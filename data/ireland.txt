Ireland:

No Poverty:
Achieved: 
- In 2024, 0.05% of the population was making $3.65/day (living in extreme poverty)
- 9.70% of the population lives in poverty after taxes and transfers

Zero Hunger:
Achieved:
- 0.7% of children under 5 y/o considered underweight for their height(s)
- 2.5% of population is considered undernourished
Major Challenges Remaining:
- 28.35% of the adult population is considered obese (BMI>=30)
- More efficient use of Nitrogen (on a scale from 0-1, Ireland scored 0.94)
- Exports of hazardous pesticides 

Good Health and Well-Being:
Achieved: 
- Maternal - Mortality ratio of 4.98 per 100000 live births
- Age standardized death rates due to pollution is 13 per 100000 people
- 2.8 traffic deaths per 100000 people
- Subjective health is rated as 6.82/10 (survey based)
	
Quality Education:
Achieved: 
- (Lower secondary education) Middle school completion rate is 100.05%
- 63.27% of people of age 25-34 attain a higher education (i.e. Bachelor's, Masters, etc.)
Challenges:
- Students who are sub-par in math is 19.03% of 15-year-olds

Climate Action:
Achieved:
- CO2 emissions from fossil fuel exports is ~21.83 kg per capita
Major Challenges Remaining:
- CO2 emissions from fossil fuels is about 7.75 tons of CO2 per capita
- Greenhouse Gas emissions is 14.14 tCO2 per capita
