Jamaica:

No Poverty:
Achieved: 
- In 2024, 1.99% of the population was making $2.15/day (living in extreme poverty)
Major Challenges remaining: 
- In 2024, 7.22% of the population was making $3.65/day (living in extreme poverty)

Zero Hunger:
Achieved:
- 3.2% of children under 5 y/o considered underweight for their height(s)
- Exports of hazardous pesticides 
Major Challenges Remaining:
- 8.3% of population is considered undernourished
- 33.81% of the adult population is considered obese (BMI>=30)
- More efficient use of Nitrogen (on a scale from 0-1, Jamaica scored 0.85)

Good Health and Well-Being:
Achieved: 
- 99.8% of births are attended by skilled health personnel
- 3.2/100000 population have tuberculosis
Challenges remaining:
- 17.8 traffic deaths per 100000 people
- Age standardized death rates due to pollution is 36 per 100000 people
- Maternal - Mortality ratio of 98.88 per 100000 live births
- Subjective health is rated as 5.87/10 (survey based)
	
Quality Education:
Achieved: 
- Students who are sub-par in math is 11.96% of 15-year-olds
- Participation rate in pre-primary school programs is 93.61% of children aged 4-6
Challenges:
- (Lower secondary education) Middle school completion rate is 84.45%
- Net primary school enrollment rate is 90.42%

Climate Action:
Achieved:
- CO2 emissions from fossil fuel exports is ~0 kg per capita
Major Challenges Remaining:
- CO2 emissions from fossil fuels is about 2.29 tons of CO2 per capita
- Greenhouse Gas emissions is 2.68 tCO2 per capita
