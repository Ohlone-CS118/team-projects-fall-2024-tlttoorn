Thailand:

No Poverty:
Achieved: 
- In 2024, 0.95% of the population was making $3.65/day (living in extreme poverty)

Zero Hunger:
Achieved:
- 5.2% of population is considered undernourished
Major Challenges Remaining:
- 7.7% of children under 5 y/o considered underweight for their height(s)
- 15.38% of the adult population is considered obese (BMI>=30)
- More efficient use of Nitrogen (on a scale from 0-1, Thailand scored 0.82)
- Exports of hazardous pesticides 

Good Health and Well-Being:
Achieved: 
- Maternal - Mortality ratio of 28.6 per 100000 live births
- Subjective health is rated as 6.28/10 (survey based)
Challenges remaining:
- 25.4 traffic deaths per 100000 people
- Age standardized death rates due to pollution is 46 per 100000 people
	
Quality Education:
Achieved: 
- (Lower secondary education) Middle school completion rate is 132.31%
- Net primary school enrollment rate is 97.7%
- Participation rate in pre-primary school programs is 98.19% of children aged 4-6

Climate Action:
Major Challenges Remaining:
- CO2 emissions from fossil fuels is about 3.78 tons of CO2 per capita
- Greenhouse Gas emissions is 2.19 tCO2 per capita
