Turkiye:

No Poverty:
Achieved: 
- In 2024, 1.12% of the population was making $3.65/day (living in extreme poverty)
Major Challenges remaining: 
- 14.70% of the population lives in poverty after taxes and transfers

Zero Hunger:
Achieved:
- 1.7% of children under 5 y/o considered underweight for their height(s)
- 2.5% of population is considered undernourished
Major Challenges Remaining:
- 33.3% of the adult population is considered obese (BMI>=30)
- More efficient use of Nitrogen (on a scale from 0-1, Turkiye scored 0.64)
- Exports of hazardous pesticides 

Good Health and Well-Being:
Achieved: 
- Maternal - Mortality ratio of 17.33 per 100000 live births
- 6.5 traffic deaths per 100000 people
Challenges remaining:
- Age standardized death rates due to pollution is 46 per 100000 people
- Subjective health is rated as 5.46/10 (survey based)
	
Quality Education:
Achieved: 
- (Lower secondary education) Middle school completion rate is 93.36%
- 41.25% of people of age 25-34 attain a higher education (i.e. Bachelor's, Masters, etc.)
Challenges:
- Students who are sub-par in math is 38.68% of 15-year-olds
- Participation rate in pre-primary school programs is 67.75% of children aged 4-6

Climate Action:
Achieved:
- CO2 emissions from fossil fuel exports is ~9.97 kg per capita
Major Challenges Remaining:
- CO2 emissions from fossil fuels is about 5.12 tons of CO2 per capita
- Greenhouse Gas emissions is 2.67 tCO2 per capita
