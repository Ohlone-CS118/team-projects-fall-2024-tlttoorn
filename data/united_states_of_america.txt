Zero Hunger:
Achieved:
- 41.99% of the adult population is considered obese (BMI>=30)

- 2.5% of population is considered undernourished
Challenges Remaining:
- More efficient use of Nitrogen (on a scale from 0-1, US scored 0.45)

Challenges remaining:
