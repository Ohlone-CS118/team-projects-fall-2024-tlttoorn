Kazakhstan:

No Poverty:
Achieved: 
- In 2024, 0.91% of the population was making $3.65/day (living in extreme poverty)

Zero Hunger:
Achieved:
- 3.1% of children under 5 y/o considered underweight for their height(s)
- 2.5% of population is considered undernourished
Major Challenges Remaining:
- 18.38% of the adult population is considered obese (BMI>=30)
- More efficient use of Nitrogen (on a scale from 0-1, Kazakhstan scored 0.81)

Good Health and Well-Being:
Achieved: 
- Maternal - Mortality ratio of 13.38 per 100000 live births
- Subjective health is rated as 6.3/10 (survey based)
- 12.2 traffic deaths per 100000 people
Challenges remaining:
- Age standardized death rates due to pollution is 83 per 100000 people
	
Quality Education:
Achieved: 
- (Lower secondary education) Middle school completion rate is 92.19%
Challenges:
- Participation rate in pre-primary school programs is 80.5% of children aged 4-6
- Net primary school enrollment rate is 95.6%

Climate Action:
